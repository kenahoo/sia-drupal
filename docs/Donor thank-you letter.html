Dear [% Display_Name %],

Singers in Accord thanks you for your generous support of the work we do:

[% Donations %]

As you know, we are a low budget arts organization, based on a foundation of volunteerism, that values every dollar and every minute donated to our cause. It's our solid base of family and friends that continue to help our organization grow.

Your dollars were well spent this year! We continue to enjoy collaborations with new directors for every concert series. Our Fall concert, featuring a return appearance from Dr. David Mennicke, was a challenging and well executed performance of choral classics. Our February 9 & 10 concert with Dr. Peter Hendrickson featured classical and folk music from Central and South America (can you say rhumba?), and our Spring finale, May 2 & 5 with Joshua Jacobson, is a musical dialogue among many styles of Jewish music. An interesting collection of music for our interesting collection of supporters!

We also remind you that we have an ongoing fundraising opportunity through TurnStyle consignment store in Richfield, MN. TurnStyle accepts clothing, accessories, jewelry, household, small furniture, and artwork for resale. Any items you donate towards the Singers in Accord account are tax deductible. You can drop off the items yourself or contact sheri.williams@singersinaccord.org for delivery of goods. Clean out your closet for a good cause!

Happy New Year to All!

    Sheri Williams, fundraising coordinator
