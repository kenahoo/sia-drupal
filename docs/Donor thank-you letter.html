Since its founding four years ago, Singers in Accord (SIA) choral ensemble has matured into a community of singers whose love for choral music and strong value of partnership and collaboration with other nonprofits has made it a significant Twin Cities community asset. We've been able to do this because of support from donors and friends like you and for this we say Thank you!

As you may know, SIA is uniquely organized as a singer-led choral ensemble with an all-volunteer singer and leadership organizational structure. It employs no paid staff members, and all money raised goes to creating, marketing and performing our annual concerts. It costs a minimum of about $10,000 to prepare and produce each concert. With our volunteer organizational structure, we have intentionally placed most of our energies and time into developing the musical and operational sides of the organization, using word of mouth and limited marketing to spread the word and recruit financial and volunteer support for our long term sustainability.

It is time to more decisively broaden our sources and methods of securing financial support and as such have embarked on a drive to establish an Accord Concert Fund. Since we begin planning our concert schedules 18-24 months out, the Accord Concert Fund will give us a financial base to operate from and help us leverage funding from new grants and businesses, many of which require matching dollars for support provided.

Please consider a generous contribution to the Accord Concert Fund and help us build the sustainability needed to ensure a strong future for SIA. On behalf of the entire ensemble I thank you for helping us to launch our fifth Season and the Accord Concert Fund.
